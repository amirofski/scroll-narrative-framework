# Narrative Design Guide

This document teaches AI agents how to design high-quality narrative experiences with SNF.

## Workflow
1. Understand the user's intent.
2. Identify the emotional journey.
3. Divide the story into sequences.
4. Define cues and behaviors.
5. Choose appropriate media (text, image, video, lottie, 3D).
6. Optimize for accessibility and performance.
7. Produce a Narrative Definition instead of runtime-specific code.

## Principles
- Story before animation.
- Motion must reinforce meaning.
- Scroll is only one possible progress driver.
- Every motion should have narrative purpose.
- Avoid decorative animation.

## AI Checklist
- Is the story understandable?
- Does every sequence have a purpose?
- Are pauses intentionally designed?
- Is reduced-motion supported?
- Can another renderer execute the same narrative?
